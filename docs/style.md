# Design System: Civic Apple (System 7 Inspired)

This design system blends the nostalgic, inviting utility of Apple System 7 with a subtle, patriotic American civic aesthetic. The goal is to create an interface that feels "official" yet warm, approachable, and trustworthyâ€”avoiding the cold starkness of modern brutalist or ultra-minimalist designs.

## Core Philosophy
-   **Subtle & Inviting**: Use warmth in colors and typography to welcome users.
-   **Civic Flavor**: Hints of Red, White, and Blue, but desaturated and used as accents, not overwhelming floods of color.
-   **System 7 Utility**: Clean lines, readable text, window-like containers, and distinct interactive elements.
-   **Clarity over Flash**: Information density is managed through whitespace and clear hierarchy, not hiding elements.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors (Civic Accents)
-   **Liberty Blue**: `#4A6FA5` (A softer, slate-leaning navy)
-   **Old Glory Red**: `#D32F2F` (A muted, brick-leaning red)
-   **Paper White**: `#FAFAFA` (Main background, not stark white)
-   **Cream**: `#FEFDF5` (Subtle tint for highlighted areas or cards)

### Neutrals (System 7 Grays)
-   **Platinum**: `#E5E5E5` (Borders, window backgrounds)
-   **Charcoal**: `#333333` (Primary text)
-   **Slate**: `#666666` (Secondary text)

## Typography

### Headings / UI Chrome
-   **Font**: *Chicago* (Classic Apple System 7 font).
-   **Style**: Distinctive pixel-sans aesthetic. Used for headers, buttons, and inputs.

### Body / Content
-   **Font**: Sans-Serif (e.g., *Inter*, *Geneva*, *Helvetica*).
-   **Style**: Clean, legible, high contrast.

## Component Styling

### Containers & Cards
-   **Background**: White or very light gray/cream.
-   **Border**: 1px solid `#E5E5E5` or `#D1D5DB`.
-   **Shadow**: Very subtle, barely there (e.g., `shadow-sm`).
-   **Corners**: Rounded (4px - 6px).

### Buttons
-   **Primary**: Liberty Blue background, White text. Rounded corners (4px). Subtle hover effect (lighten).
-   **Secondary**: White background, Platinum border, Charcoal text.
-   **Accent**: Old Glory Red for destructive or attention-grabbing actions.

### Imagery
-   **Motifs**: Subtle use of stars, stripes, or eagle iconography in icons or empty states, but abstracted and minimal.
-   **Texture**: Grain or paper texture can be used sparingly in backgrounds.
